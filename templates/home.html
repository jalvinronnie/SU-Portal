Students' Union Portal

About SUC

The Student Union Council is one of the most important student bodies on campus. Its main purpose is to cater to the needs of Students as a whole and bring it to the notice of the Administration be it hostel problems, sanitation on campus or even related to the mess food. It serves as a bridge between the concerned authorities and all students on campus including the higher degree ones. It was established with a vision of providing a student an easy way to bring about changes through the administration.

The President

The President, the Head of the Union and the Council, has the power to speak on behalf of the Union with any authority without prior consultation with other members of the Council. He is also the signing authority of all the financial matters and statements of the Union as he is elected by the Students themselves.

The General Secretary

Below the President is the General Secretary, accountable for all the inventory of all the property owned by the Union’s organs. He takes care of the general affairs of the college and is responsible for all meetings of the Council, keeping minutes of the same

The Technical Secretary

The Technical Secretary is accountable for the technical activities and culture on the campus and organizes any intra-college technical fests.

The Sports Secretaries

The Sports Secretaries help in nurturing and developing sports culture on campus and also organize the intra college sports fests.

The Cultural Secretaries

The Cultural Secretaries co-ordinate all cultural activities of the campus and generally take up the organizing of those events along with those of the intra-college cultural fest.

The Hostel Representatives

The Hostel Representatives, elected by each hostel’s students, focus on the development along with maintenance of the hostel facilities and monitor the inventory in their respective hostels.
